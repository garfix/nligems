A natural language system by the creator of Prolog, Alain Colmerauer.
Written in Prolog, it answers questions about space, in French and English.

~~~
H: Who discovered Pluto?
C: Tombaugh

H: Quel est le diametre de Leda ?
C: 140 km
~~~
